Abburn — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

Abburn is built to respect your privacy completely. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with information.

Summary

Abburn does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Information we collect

None. Abburn has no servers, no backend, and makes no network requests. Nothing you do in the app is sent anywhere.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences — workout durations, selected theme, and any custom workout you build — are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard storage (UserDefaults). This information never leaves your device, is never accessible to us or any third party, and is permanently removed when you delete the app.

Third-party services and tracking

Abburn contains no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks. It does not track you across other apps or websites and does not access the device advertising identifier. The app works fully offline.

Children's privacy

Abburn does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Exercise imagery

Exercise demonstration images are provided by Everkinetic and used under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 (CC BY-SA 3.0) license. This is an attribution notice only and involves no collection or sharing of your data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
