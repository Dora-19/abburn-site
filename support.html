Quick answers

No sound? Check your device isn't muted and the volume is up. Abburn's beeps play through the media channel.

Screen dimming mid-workout? Abburn keeps the screen awake while a timer is running; make sure the workout is started (not paused).

My custom workout disappeared? Custom workouts are stored on your device and are removed if the app is deleted.
